02 · 取捨公式:Brilliance per Dollar

BrillianceLab 把這個取捨整理成一條公式 — 我們稱之為 BPD(Brilliance per Dollar,每元閃光值):

BPD = ( S × C0.5 × η(色,淨) ) / P萬元
 
S:BrillianceLab 光學分數(0–100)
C:克拉(視覺面積 ∝ √C,因此用 C0.5)
η:顏色 × 淨度的視覺折扣係數(D=1.00 / G=0.97 / J=0.85,VVS1=1.00 / VS1=0.99 / SI1=0.94)
P:鑽石價格(NT$ / 萬元)

這條公式比常見的「分數 × 克拉 / 價格」多了兩件事:

  • 用 √C 取代 C:鑽石的視覺感受面積是寬度平方,寬度 ≈ ∛克拉 × 直徑常數,所以視覺尺寸跟克拉成 √C 比例,而不是線性。1.0 ct 看起來只比 0.7 ct 大 ~20%,而非 ~43%。
  • η 折扣係數,而非「等級分數」:G 與 D 的視覺差距其實只有 3%,但價差可達 40%。線性的等級分數高估了顏色的影響。

07 · 常見問題

Q1:0.85 克拉看起來會不會比一克拉小很多?

不會。圓形鑽石的實際視覺直徑差距極小:一克拉約 6.5 mm,0.85 克拉約 6.2 mm,差距只有 5%(差不多就是一根頭髮的寬度)。並排比較幾乎看不出來,但 0.85 克拉的真八心八箭 明顯比一克拉的邊緣 Excellent 更閃亮。

圖 30.85 克拉與一克拉的視覺直徑只差 0.3 mm . 約一根頭髮的寬度

Q2:車工真的比顏色重要這麼多嗎?

是的。鑽石被稱為「光的玩具」,所有美感都來自光的反射。車工不好的鑽石無論顏色多白、淨度多乾淨,都不會閃。反之,真 H&A 的鑽石就算 G 色也會看起來像 D 色一樣晶亮。

Q3:這條 BPD 公式只適用於圓形嗎?

是的,本站的演算法只針對圓形明亮式 (Round Brilliant)調校。橢圓、公主方、墊形、馬眼等異形切工有自己的光學最佳比例,目前不在範圍內。日後會擴充。

Q4:如果我已經買了「邊緣 Excellent」怎麼辦?

不要焦慮。GIA Excellent 仍然是業界前 5% 的車工,它依然很美。下次升級時(週年、紀念日)再用 BPD 公式挑就好。

02 · The trade-off formula: Brilliance per Dollar

BrillianceLab packages this trade-off into one formula — BPD (Brilliance per Dollar):

BPD = ( S × C0.5 × η(color, clarity) ) / P(per NT$10K)
 
S: BrillianceLab optical score (0–100)
C: carat (visual area ∝ √C, hence the square root)
η: color × clarity visual discount factor (D=1.00 / G=0.97 / J=0.85; VVS1=1.00 / VS1=0.99 / SI1=0.94)
P: price per NT$10,000 unit

Two things make this different from the usual "score × carat / price":

  • √C instead of C — visual area scales with the square of the diameter, and diameter scales with ∛C × constant, which means perceived size scales with √C, not linearly. 1.00 ct only looks ~20% bigger than 0.70 ct face-up.
  • η discount, not "tier score" — the visible difference between G and D is ~3%, but price difference can be 40%. Tier-based scores massively overweight color.

06 · FAQ

Q1. Will 0.85 ct look much smaller than 1.00 ct?

No. Round brilliant 1.00 ct ≈ 6.5 mm; 0.85 ct ≈ 6.2 mm — 5% diameter delta. Side-by-side it's invisible; face-up, the H&A 0.85 ct simply looks brighter.

Q2. Is cut really that much more important than color?

Yes. A diamond is a "toy for light" — all of its beauty comes from reflection. A poorly cut diamond won't sparkle no matter how white or clean. A true H&A G-color reads as bright as a D-color.

Q3. Does the BPD formula apply to non-round shapes?

No — only to round brilliants. Ovals, princess, cushion, marquise have their own optical optima. Future versions of BrillianceLab will add them.

Q4. I already bought a borderline Excellent — what now?

Don't panic. GIA Excellent is still the top 5% of cuts in the market, and your stone is still beautiful. Use BPD for your next upgrade (anniversary, eternity).
